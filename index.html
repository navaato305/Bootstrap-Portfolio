About Me

I grew up in Miami, Florida learning music and using computers and other technologeis. From the third grade to twelfth I was in the band and orchestra playing upright bass. I applied for All State in seventh grade, which is a national music competition where only the best get in, and got accepted and continued to attend for the next five years.

I've always been fast at picking up technology and interested in learning more about it, so I began teaching myself how to understand troubleshooting for PC and then for internet routers. Only recently did I start to learn to program and immerse myself in this new world of tech.

Currently I'm taking a programming bootcamp course that will teach me all about front end and back end development. I have tried before to learn by myself, but unfortunately I couldn't learn very well on my own and am very much looking forward to this class so I can finally understand everything that I've wanted to.
